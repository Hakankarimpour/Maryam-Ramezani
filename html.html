About me

I am Maryam Ramezani.
I graduated from Tarbiat Badani High School and have a Master's degree in Exercise Physiology from the University of Guilan. I have been coaching for 10 years and have 3 years of experience in the Premier League as a strength and conditioning coach for the Sanat Mes Rafsanjan volleyball team. My specialties include strength and conditioning for fitness, weight loss and gain, and strength and conditioning for sports such as volleyball and futsal. Also, if you have a specific issue, I can help alleviate your pain with the advice of a physical therapist.
